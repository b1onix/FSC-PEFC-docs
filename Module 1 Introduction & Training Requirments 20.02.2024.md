Chain of Custody
Training process
•Participate in a refresher training covering CoC & EUDR DDS
•Pass the CoC + EUDR knowledge test, minimum score of 80%
Enrolled to take the knowledge tests.
Each link is personal. The test shall be taken by the trainee, according to the email provided during
the registration
1 monthto take it, from the moment you receive the email
One must pass the CoC knowledge test to unlock the EUDR DDS
If score less than 80%:
oBetween 65% and 80%: review the training materials and standards and re-take the
knowledge test
oLess than 65%: the participant will need to re-take the training and the knowledge test

Chain of Custody
PEFC Knowledge Academy
•You will receive an email from
the PEFC Knowledge
Academy alerting you of your
enrolment.
•Click on the link in your email
to log in and access the
knowledge test.
•You will have access for a
maximum of 1 month until you
finish the tests.

PEFC CoC Training Validity
•Each refresher/initial training
certificate is valid for 2 years
•If the training took place
between 01/01202X and
30/06/202X then it will expire
on 30/06/202X+2, i.e. two
years later end June
•If the training took place
between 01/07/202X and
31/12/202X then it will expire
on 31/12/202X+2, i.e. two
years later end December
