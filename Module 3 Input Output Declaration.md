## Recycled Material
What is accepted as PEFC recycled material?
i) Recovered from waste during a manufacturing
process.  Noreutilisation of material capable of
being reclaimed within the same process
ii) Generated by end-users that the product can no
longer be used for its intended purpose. See ISO
14021:1999 lifecycle of materials
Material delivered with a recycled claim from
another certification system. It needs to meet
PEFCs definition of recycled material
Examples of documents & claims that could be
accepted as recycled material: EN 643
classification, Product details, Producer claim,
Legal statements, ISO 14021 claims, A Type iii
Environmental Product Declaration, compliant
with UNI EN 15804 and ISO 14025 (EPD)
More examples on GD 3.35
