PSU Derogation
Code  DER-STD-20-001_01 V1-1-amended
Requirement (s)  Clause 4.7.1 of FSC-STD-20-001
Request by CB
Is it acceptable to postpone the audit of a certificate holder by one month to the
next calendar year, if the audit takes place within the 15 months after the last
audit?
Rationale as pro-
vided by CBs
The audit was not feasible for reasons beyond the control of the certificate
holder and the CB.
PSU conclusion
The audit may be postponed by one month to the next calendar year within the
limits of the 15 months rule. All other requirements including the requirement to
have 4 surveillance audits within one certification cycle remain unaffected.
This derogation is granted for cases that are beyond the control of the certifi-
cate holder and the CB (such as sickness and natural hazards like e.g. fire and
flooding).
The CB shall record each case in the respective certification report.

tion
