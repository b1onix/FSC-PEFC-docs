Geographical scope of PEFC notification by the PEFC Council


## Version 4
(November 2024)


The scope of the PEFC Council notification contract covers PEFC Chain of Custody certification of
entities registered in other countries than listed in the table below. The table provides the list of
countries where PEFC National Members are administering the PEFC certification scheme.

In case of multi-national multisite certification PEFC Council notification covers certificates issued to
multisite organisations which head office is located in a country under PEFC Council geographical
scope.

Notification for PEFC certification in countries below can be obtained from the PEFC authorised
bodies. Their contact details are available on the PEFC Council website (
https://pefc.org/discover-
pefc/our-pefc-members/national-members) and on request at info@pefc.org.
