## A  Objective

The objective of this document is to provide references of normative documents to be used
for FSC controlled wood certification by stakeholders.
## B Scope

This addendum lists  the  national  and  regional  risk  assessments  that  have  been
developed in accordance with the process specified in FSC-PRO-60-002 and have been
evaluated and approved by FSC.
C Effective and validity dates

Approval date 10 November 2014
Publication date 01 December 2014
Effective date 01 January 2015
Period of validity until 31 December 2019 (or until replaced or withdrawn)

This document will be updated as new documents are approved.
Dates of updates of this document are specified below.
## D References
No documents are indispensable for the application of this procedure addendum.
This addendum however is indispensable for the application of other normative
documents (FSC-STD-40-005 Requirements for Sourcing FSC Controlled Wood).
E Terms and definitions

For the purpose of this procedure, the terms and definitions provided in FSC-STD-
01-002 FSC Glossary of terms, FSC-PRO-06-002 The Development and approval of
FSC National Risk Assessments and FSC-STD-40-005 Requirements for Sourcing
FSC Controlled Wood apply.

1 Document flow

1.1 An  NRA  approved  according  to FSC-PRO-60-002  V2-0 (‘old NRA’) remains
valid  until  replaced  by  an  FSC risk  assessment approved  according  to FSC-
PRO-60-002 V3-0, but no longer than until 30 June 2019.

NOTE: National risk assessments approved according to version 2-0 of FSC-PRO-60-
002 are listed in Annex A of this procedure addendum.

1.2 If an ‘old NRA’ is not replaced by the FSC risk assessment approved
according to FSC-PRO-60-002 V3-0 by 30 June 2019, the area covered in
the ‘old NRA’ becomes unassessed area. Organizations sourcing material
from this area will be required to develop extended company risk
assessments (ECRA) instead.

1.3 Approved  FSC  risk  assessments  are  available  on  the  FSC document  center
## (https://fsc.org/en/document-centre).
