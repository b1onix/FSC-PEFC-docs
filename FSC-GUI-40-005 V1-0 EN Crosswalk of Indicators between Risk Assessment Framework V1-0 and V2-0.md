## INTRODUCTION
This guidance is an output of analysis, comparing the indicators specified in <FSC-PRO-60-006b Risk
Assessment Framework> against those specified in <FSC-PRO-60-002a FSC National Risk Assessment
Framework> and the requirements established in the EU Regulation on Deforestation-free Products
## (EUDR 2023/1115).
## 1

The main objective of this analysis was to understand how the indicators of the revised Risk Assessment
Framework cover requirements specified under indicators in <FSC-PRO-60-002a FSC National Risk
Assessment Framework> and the alignment with EUDR.


## 1
Source: Regulation (EU) 2023/1115 of the European Parliament and of the Council on the making available on the Union
market and the export from the Union of certain commodities and products associated with deforestation and forest degradation
and repealing Regulation (EU) No 995/2010.

## RISK ASSESSMENT INDICATORS CROSSWALK
The key changes in the indicators specified in <FSC-PRO-60-006b Risk Assessment Framework> are
presented below (see table 1 for further information):
a) There are 64 indicators in comparison to the 32 indicators of the previous version of the
Framework (<FSC-PRO-60-002a FSC National Risk Assessment Framework>).
b) 57 indicators were part of previous version of the Framework. Minor adjustments have been
made to go beyond the legal requirements and assess the sustainability aspects. This implies
89% of the requirements retained to ensure stability of the system.
c) 5 new indicators included to cover the <FSC-POL-01-007 Policy to Address Conversion> and
## EUDR.
d) 2 new indicators included to assess sustainability aspects, aligned with FSC’s values focusing on:
i. minimize the adverse impact on environmental values from development and maintenance of
infrastructure, and ii. workers' health and safety.
